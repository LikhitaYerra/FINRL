The Problem

Markets are driven by news and narrative, yet most RL agents see only price/technical data.

📰
News is Noisy

Millions of financial articles daily — no scalable way to quantify their market impact without LLMs.

📉
Bear Markets Kill Agents

Standard PPO agents ignore downside risk, suffering deep drawdowns in 2020, 2022 crashes.

🔀
One-size-fits-all Fails

Bull markets need return maximisation; bear markets need capital preservation. Different agents needed.

Our solution: Use DeepSeek-V3 to extract 4 structured signals per news article, feed them into a risk-sensitive CPPO agent, and switch to PPO in bull regimes.

Contest Evaluation Criteria

FinRL Contest 2025 · Task 1 · Model evaluation (60%) + Paper (40%)

Metric 1 Cumulative Return

Total portfolio return over the 2019–2023 test period. Higher is better.

Metric 2 Rachev Ratio

E[top 5% returns] / E[bottom 5% losses]. Captures asymmetric upside vs tail risk.

Metric 3 Max Drawdown

Worst peak-to-trough portfolio decline. Lower is better — measures downside risk control.

Metric 4 Outperformance Frequency

% of days beating NASDAQ-100, especially during market downturns.

Our CPPO-MultiSignal + Regime-Switch is optimised for all four metrics simultaneously

ACTION MODULATION

Sentiment
Strong buy signal → amplify matching actions × 1.1
Mismatch → reduce × 0.9
Confidence
scale = 1 + 0.02 × (confidence − 3)
High confidence amplifies the sentiment effect
Volatility Forecast
scale = 1 − 0.05 × (vol − 3)  ∈ [0.8, 1.2]
High volatility → tighter positions
Drawdown Penalty
r_shaped = r − λ × |r| × (peak − value)/peak
Directly optimises max-drawdown metric

Constrained PPO (CPPO)

CPPO adds a CVaR Lagrange constraint on top of PPO, penalising trajectories that exceed the risk budget when LLM risk is high.

# LLM-weighted risk factor
weights = {1:0.99, 2:0.995, 3:1.0, 4:1.005, 5:1.01}
llm_risk_factor = dot(stock_weights, risk_weights)

# Risk-adjusted trajectory value
D_π = llm_risk_factor × (ep_ret + V − r)

# CVaR penalty when below threshold
if D_π < ν:
  updates = λ/(1−α) × (ν − D_π)
Key hyperparameters
α = 0.85 (CVaR level) · β = 3000 · γ = 0.995
clip_ratio = 0.7 · 100 epochs · 20k steps/epoch

Regime-Switching Agent

Key insight from paper: "Bull market → PPO  |  Bear market → CPPO-DeepSeek"
We automate this with a real-time regime detector.

REGIME DETECTION LOGIC (regime_switch_agent.py)

# Calibrated on training period (2013–2018)
turb_threshold = percentile75(train_turbulence)
vix_threshold = percentile66(trade_VIX)

def act(obs, turbulence, vix):
  if turbulence ≥ turb_threshold
    or vix ≥ vix_threshold:
    return cppo_model.act(obs) # bear
  return ppo_model.act(obs) # bull

WHY CPPO-MULTISIGNAL WINS ON RACHEV

Drawdown penalty in reward
Directly suppresses the downside tail during training → smaller denominator.
Confidence signal amplification
High-confidence positive news → bigger buys → fatter right tail.
Volatility dampening
Pre-emptively reduces exposure before volatile events, avoiding crash spikes.
CPPO CVaR constraint
Hard-limits the expected shortfall in worst-case trajectories.
