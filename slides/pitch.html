02 — The Problem

Markets move on news.
RL agents are completely blind to it.

📰
Information gap

Millions of market-moving articles every day — invisible to price-only RL agents that only see numbers.

📉
Bear-market collapse

Standard PPO suffers 30–40% drawdowns in the 2020 and 2022 crashes with no risk safeguard at all.

🔀
One policy fails both

Bull markets need return maximisation. Bear markets need capital preservation. A single fixed policy can't do both.

The NASDAQ-100 dropped 34% in 33 days in March 2020. A risk-aware agent reading the news could have seen it coming.

04 — How It Works

Three layers.
One smart agent.

🧠
Layer 1 — LLM Intelligence score_multi_signal.py

DeepSeek-V3 reads every financial news article and outputs 4 structured scores per article. These become extra dimensions in the agent's observation vector — it literally reads the news before deciding what to trade.

4
signals
⚙️
Layer 2 — Risk-Sensitive RL train_cppo_multi_signal.py

Constrained PPO with a CVaR Lagrange multiplier and a drawdown penalty baked into the reward. High LLM risk scores dynamically tighten the CVaR budget, forcing the agent to go conservative exactly when it matters.

CVaR
constraint
🔀
Layer 3 — Regime Switching regime_switch_agent.py

Every day: if turbulence index or VIX crosses a calibrated threshold → hand control to CPPO (bear mode). Otherwise → PPO (bull mode). Two specialists, always the right one in charge.

agents

06 — LLM Signal · Live Example

One prompt. Four signals. Every article.

Prompt sent to DeepSeek-V3
System: Output 4 scores separated by |:
  sentiment | risk | confidence | volatility
  Scale 1–5. No explanation. One line per item.

User:
Stock: AAPL | Apple beats earnings by 20%
Stock: NVDA | GPU shortage slows data centers
Stock: META | CEO faces Senate hearing on AI

Assistant:
5|1|5|2  ← AAPL: great news, low risk, very confident, calm
2|4|3|4  ← NVDA: bad news, high risk, neutral conf., volatile
2|3|2|3  ← META: negative, moderate risk, uncertain
Parsed signal table
Ticker Sentiment Risk Confidence Volatility
AAPL 5 ↑ 1 5 2
NVDA 2 ↓ 4 3 4
META 2 ↓ 3 2 3
How signals modulate trades
AAPL: high sentiment × high confidence → amplified buy
NVDA: low sentiment × high volatility → position scaled down
META: low confidence → hold, don't act on weak signal
07 — Regime Switching

Bull market → step on the gas.
Bear market → hit the brakes.

Detection logic
TURBULENCE INDEX

Daily turbulence index ≥ 75th percentile of training period → bear mode activated. Quantifies abnormal co-movement between assets.

VIX (FEAR INDEX)

VIX ≥ 66th percentile → market fear is elevated → CPPO takes control. Thresholds calibrated on 2013–2018 training data.

RESULT

~75% of days in bull mode (PPO) · ~25% in bear mode (CPPO). Always the right specialist.
